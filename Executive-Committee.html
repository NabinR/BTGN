Executive Committee

BTGN’s Executive Committee is the peak governing body of BTGN. It sets the direction and oversees the organisation. It holds the authority and responsibility for leadership, oversight, integrity and quality for all of the operations of BTGN. The Committee comprises up to twenty-one members who are elected every two years. In addition, up to seven members can be selected as Invited Members by the executive committee, based on their interest, expertise and contributions. These Invited Members do not have voting rights.
