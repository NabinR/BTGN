Become a Member

BTGN greatly values the contribution of interested and committed individuals and groups. For those who wish to become a member of BTGN, please follow the steps below.

  1. Complete the Membership Application Form
    BTGN Secretary will review your application and get back to you as soon as possible.
  2. Conversation face to face or via telephone, Skype, or Viber
  3. BTGN decision on your membership
  4. Payment of Membership Fee via PayPal
  5. Participation in BTGN events and general assembly
